## 2. What ClearDrive is — and is not

ClearDrive is a **facilitation platform** for used-vehicle sale transactions between a Seller and a Buyer in the United Arab Emirates. ClearDrive:

- Coordinates document generation (Transaction/Escrow Agreement, Limited Power of Attorney, and related deal documents).
- Coordinates identity verification (KYC) of both parties.
- Coordinates electronic signature of deal documents via a third-party e-signature provider.
- Coordinates fund escrow, loan settlement, and traffic-fine settlement via a licensed, regulated third-party escrow partner.
- Coordinates the RTA (Roads and Transport Authority) ownership transfer ("Tasjeel") step, which remains the responsibility of the Seller and Buyer to attend in person as required by RTA.

**ClearDrive does not**: hold client funds directly, act as a bank, act as a licensed escrow agent itself, provide legal advice, guarantee vehicle condition or title accuracy beyond RTA/fines data retrieved, or act as a party to the underlying sale contract between Seller and Buyer.
