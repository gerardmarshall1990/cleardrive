# ClearDrive — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [DATE]

This Privacy Policy explains how **LUXE F.Z.E** (trading as "ClearDrive") collects, uses, stores, and shares personal data when you use the ClearDrive web app, mobile app, and related services (together, the "Platform"), in accordance with the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data ("PDPL").

## 1. Data we collect

- **Identity data**: full name, Emirates ID number, nationality, phone number, email address.
- **KYC data**: identity verification results and status (processed via our KYC/escrow partner's UAE Pass integration).
- **Vehicle & deal data**: plate number, VIN, make/model/year, mileage, sale price, outstanding loan details, traffic fine amounts.
- **Financial data**: seller IBAN and receiving bank account name, for the sole purpose of proceeds disbursement via our licensed Escrow Partner. ClearDrive does not store card numbers or process payments directly.
- **Documents**: generated deal documents (Transaction/Escrow Agreement, Power of Attorney, RTA transfer certificate, fines screenshots) and their signature status.
- **Communications data**: WhatsApp messages and emails sent to you regarding your deal, and their delivery status.
- **Technical data**: IP address, device/browser type, and log data generated by using the Platform.

## 2. How we use this data

We use your data to: create and progress your vehicle sale deal through each stage; verify your identity; generate and route deal documents for signature; instruct our Escrow Partner to hold and disburse funds and settle loans/fines; send you deal status updates via WhatsApp/email; and comply with our own legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with unrelated third parties.

## 3. Who we share data with

- **TrustIn Financial Technologies LLC** (our ADGM/FSRA-regulated Escrow Partner) — to hold and disburse sale proceeds, and settle outstanding loans/fines.
- **Our e-signature provider** — to route deal documents for electronic signature.
- **Our WhatsApp Business messaging provider** and **email delivery provider** — to send you deal-related notifications.
- **RTA** (Roads and Transport Authority) — where applicable, in connection with the ownership transfer step.
- Law enforcement or regulators, where required by UAE law.
